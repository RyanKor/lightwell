APOLLO 200 is an IGBT type DC Arc and TIG (scratch type) welder; it is small sized and light weighted for portable use in case of dispatched work and electric shock prevention function is provided for the safety of users.

Input Electricity

220V single phase 50/60Hz

Output Current

10~18A

Input Current

5KVA

Rated Load Voltage

25V

No-Load Voltage

70V

Rate of Use

70%(25° outside)

Dimensions

140 x 330 x 240mm

Weight (Net)

Less than 10Kg
 

  • Convenient Carrying – Carrying is convenient for dispatched work because the size is very small and the weight is light.
  • Front/Back Plastic Cases are used for improvement of carrying and safety.
  • High Rate of Use – Rated rate of use is possible up to 60% at 200A compared with the size of the cases.
  • Circuit Protection with NFB – NFB is applied for input power and the circuit is automatically and rapidly broken in case of internal trouble or leakage.
  • Use of Various Rods – Various kinds of metals and coating flux may be used.
